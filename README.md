# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard: Workforce & Attrition Insights

## 📋 Project Overview
This project delivers a comprehensive **Power BI Business Intelligence solution** designed for HR managers and executive leadership. The dashboard transforms raw employee data into actionable insights, specifically focusing on **attrition patterns**, workforce demographics, and employee satisfaction.

By leveraging trendlines and interactive filtering, this tool enables data-driven decision-making to improve retention strategies and foster a more inclusive corporate environment.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Power BI:** Data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive visualization.
* **Advanced Excel:** Data cleaning, ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes, and initial data profiling.
* **Statistics:** Descriptive statistics to identify significant trends and attrition drivers.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard provides an immediate snapshot of organizational health through five primary metrics:
* **Employee Count:** Total workforce size.
* **Attrition Count:** Total number of employees who have departed.
* **Attrition Rate:** The percentage of turnover relative to the total headcount.
* **Active Employees:** Current standing workforce.
* **Average Age:** Demographic maturity of the organization.

---

## 🔍 Data Visualizations & Insights

### 1. Gender-Based Attrition (Lollipop Chart)
Provides a high-contrast comparison between male and female turnover rates to identify potential gender-specific retention issues.

### 2. Departmental Breakdown (Pie Chart)
Identifies "at-risk" departments by visualizing the proportion of total attrition occurring in each business unit.

### 3. Demographic Distribution (Bar Graph)
Segments the workforce into age bands, allowing HR to plan for generational shifts and targeted engagement.

### 4. Job Satisfaction Analysis (Square Chart)
A heat-map style square chart that visualizes employee sentiment across the organization to correlate satisfaction with turnover.

### 5. Educational Impact (Vertical Bar & Donut Charts)
Cross-references attrition with educational backgrounds to see if specific recruitment sources or fields of study correlate with higher churn.
